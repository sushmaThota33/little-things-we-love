# 5 reasons to use Little Things We Love ❤️

1. **Notice the little things again.** Daily life makes you stop seeing what's right in front of you — this app trains you to look for them.

2. **Long-distance feels less long.** Each note is a tiny "I'm thinking of you across the miles" — and on the 3rd, you get a whole month of them at once.

3. **Breaks the autopilot of living together.** When everything feels routine, this forces a daily pause to actually appreciate your person.

4. **Brings back the dating-era romance for married couples.** "I love you" turns into specifics again — the small things they did, the way they laughed, the things that still make you fall.

5. **Truly private — no audience, no algorithm.** End-to-end encrypted, no likes, no posts. Just for the two of you.
