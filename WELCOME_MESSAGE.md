# Little Things We Love ❤️

**Little Things We Love** is a tiny shared diary just for couples. You and your partner each write little notes throughout the month — sweet things, appreciations, tiny moments — and the notes stay hidden until the 3rd of next month, when you both unlock them and read everything together. It's a month-long love time-capsule.

🔗 **App:** https://little-things-we-love.vercel.app/login

### How to use it

- **Sign up separately** — each partner makes their own account with email, display name, and a password.
- **Pair up** — one of you clicks _"Start a new space"_, sets a **Diary passphrase** (4 random words, 12+ characters — e.g. `orange piano coffee river`), and shares the invite link with the partner.
- **Partner joins** — they sign up, click the invite link, and enter the same passphrase. Paired! 💞
- **Write notes** anytime — from either phone, as many as you want.
- **Unlock** on the 3rd of the next month (or anytime after) — tap _"Unlock Notes 🔓"_ and read everything your partner wrote.

### 🔐 About the encryption (important)

Your notes are **end-to-end encrypted**. That means **nobody but you two can ever read them** — not the server, not the database, not even me (the developer). Everything is scrambled inside your browser before it leaves, using a key that only your passphrase can produce.

- The **Diary passphrase** is the only key. Share it with your partner through a different message than the invite link (text it, or say it aloud).
- Save it in a password manager or write it down somewhere safe.
- ⚠️ **If you both forget it, the notes are gone forever.** There's no reset — that's what makes it private.

Have fun with it 💛 Ping me if anything breaks.
