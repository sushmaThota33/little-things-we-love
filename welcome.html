Little Things We Love

a month-long love time-capsule, just for two
❤️ 💗 💕 💖 💘

Little Things We Love is a tiny shared diary just for couples. You and your partner each write little notes throughout the month — sweet things, appreciations, tiny moments — and the notes stay hidden until the 3rd of the next month, when you both unlock them together and read everything at once. 💌

💻 Open the app
https://little-things-we-love.vercel.app/login

✨ How to use it

  1. Sign up separately. Each partner makes their own account — email, display name, password.
  2. One of you creates the space. Tap "Start a new space", set a Diary passphrase (4 random words, 12+ chars — e.g. orange piano coffee river), and copy the invite link.
  3. Partner joins. They sign up, open the invite link, and type the same passphrase. Paired! 💞
  4. Write notes anytime — as many as you want, from either phone.
  5. Unlock from the 3rd of the next month onwards — tap "Unlock Notes 🔓" and read what your partner wrote for you that month. 📖

🔐 About the encryption

Your notes are end-to-end encrypted. Nobody but you two can ever read them — not the server, not the database, not even me (the developer). Everything is scrambled inside your browser before it leaves, using a key that only your Diary passphrase can produce.

  • The Diary passphrase is the only key.
  • Share it with your partner through a different message than the invite link (text it, or say it aloud).
  • Save it in a password manager or write it down somewhere safe.
⚠️ If you both forget it, the notes are gone forever. There's no reset button — that's exactly what makes the app private.
Have fun with it! Ping me if anything breaks.
